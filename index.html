Fresh Flats by Lillian Louise

Our goal is to keep your flats smelling fresh all day long.

How It Works

Essential oils are used to break down the odor producing compound produced by foot bacteria. This is not designed to be anti-bacterial or anti-fungul. Just anti-odor.

Quick Drying

Fresh Flats is designed to dry quickly.

Directrions

Try 1-2 sprays per shoe, either before or after wearing the shoes. Fresh Flats is designed in a small container to bring with you during the day incase you need to discretely freshen up your shoes.
